Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines.

Machine learning is a subset of AI that focuses on learning patterns from data.

Deep learning is a type of machine learning that uses neural networks with many layers.

Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) improves language models by retrieving relevant documents before generating answers.

Vector databases store text as numerical embeddings to enable semantic search.

Embeddings represent the meaning of text in high-dimensional space.
